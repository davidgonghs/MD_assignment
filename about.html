Best Panda Zoo

Chengdu Research Base of Panda Breeding is one of the main research bases of the Chinese government to implement the protection project of the migration of endangered wild animals such as pandas, and is a national AAAA-level tourist attraction. It is also a world-renowned wildlife protection research institution integrating scientific research and breeding of pandas, conservation education, educational tourism, and panda culture construction.

01

About Pandas

Carry out targeted scientific research activities on pandas to better breed and protect the development of the panda population.

At the same time, it also opens up sightseeing and tourism activities to the public to let people better understand pandas.

02

Tourist Notice

Please don't make loud noises, and don't make unusual, sharp noises casually, which will disturb the wild animals.

Animals such as pandas naturally like quiet surroundings and are sensitive to noise. In order to ensure the effect of your visit, please respect their living habits and maintain a peaceful environment.
